THEORY

Everything is changing all the time. The ability to sit down and learn a new skill will keep you relevent in the job market and an invaluble part of any team.

A dream is something you wish you would happen; a goal is something you intend to make happen. Goals will help you stay on track in this program and be successful in your career.

This will demonstrate your ability to set specific, measurable goals, and track your progress.

Your skills will help you keep a job; your network is what will get you a job in the first place. Meetups are a great way to connect with the local community, meet other developers and hiring managers, learn new technologies, and stay in touch with what's happening in the development community.
